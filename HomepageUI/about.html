UP Lente History

In 2014, fifteen digital art enthusiasts assembled to find and create beauty amidst the devastation following the onslaught of Super Typhoon Yolanda. UP Lente, a Multimedia Arts Guild in formerly UP Visayas Tacloban College, was born out of love and passion for the arts.

üìñ Journey

Throughout time, UP Lente has promoted social, political, cultural, and environmental awareness through multimedia arts in the region. The camaraderie, skill, and dedication of each member of UP Lente have led to the bequeathal of generations of multimedia arts enthusiasts.
